## 🎯 What's New in v2.0

### **1. Lightning-Fast Performance**
- ⚡ **40% faster load times** through advanced code splitting and lazy loading
- 📦 **<500KB initial bundle** — optimized for speed on any connection
- 🎯 **Lighthouse score 90+** across all metrics (Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO)

### **2. Complete Accessibility Overhaul**
- ♿ **WCAG 2.1 AA compliant** — accessible to all users
- ⌨️ **Full keyboard navigation** — no mouse required
- 🔊 **Screen reader optimized** — tested with NVDA and VoiceOver
- 🎨 **High contrast modes** — readable in any environment

### **3. Approval Workflows**
Transform your content creation process with multi-level approval chains:
- 📝 **Creator → Reviewer → Approver** workflow
- 📧 **Automatic email notifications** at each approval stage
- 📜 **Complete audit trail** of all approvals and changes
- 🔒 **Role-based permissions** for team security

### **4. AI-Powered Intelligence**
Harness the power of AI to optimize every post:
- 🤖 **Performance Prediction** — AI forecasts engagement before you publish
- #️⃣ **Smart Hashtag Suggestions** — context-aware recommendations based on your content
- 🎨 **Brand Voice AI** — generate on-brand content automatically
- 📊 **Optimization Tips** — real-time suggestions to improve performance

### **5. Advanced Hashtag Analytics**
Understand what drives engagement:
- 📈 **Performance metrics per hashtag** (reach, engagement, impressions)
- 📊 **Trend charts** showing performance over time
- 🏆 **Top/bottom performers** ranked by engagement
- 📅 **Date range filtering** (7d, 30d, 90d, custom)
- 💾 **Export to CSV** for deeper analysis

### **6. Enhanced Scheduling Experience**
Schedule content your way:
- 📅 **Drag-and-drop rescheduling** on calendar view
- 📦 **Bulk operations** — schedule, delete, or approve multiple posts at once
- 🔄 **Recurring posts** — set it and forget it
- ⏰ **Optimal timing suggestions** — AI recommends best posting times

### **7. Professional Media Management**
Upload and organize media effortlessly:
- 🖼️ **Drag-and-drop upload** with instant preview
- 🎬 **Multi-format support** — images, videos, GIFs
- 🔀 **Reorder media** by dragging thumbnails
- 👁️ **Real-time preview** — see exactly how posts will appear on each platform

### **8. Smart Drafts & Templates**
Never lose your work:
- 💾 **Auto-save drafts** every 30 seconds
- 📋 **Post templates** for recurring content
- 🔄 **Draft recovery** on page reload
- 📚 **Template library** with categories
