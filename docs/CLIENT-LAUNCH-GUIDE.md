## 🚀 Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to your platform URL (provided by Steel City AI)
2. Enter your credentials:

3. Click **Log In**

**First-Time Login Checklist:**

## 🔗 Connecting Your Accounts

### Supported Platforms
- Facebook (Pages)
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn (Company Pages)
- Instagram (Business Accounts)

### Connection Steps

1. **Navigate to Accounts Tab**
   - Click **Accounts** in the top navigation

2. **Add New Account**
   - Click **+ Connect Account**
   - Select your platform (e.g., Facebook)

3. **Authorize Access**
   - You'll be redirected to the platform's login page
   - Log in to your account
   - Grant the requested permissions:
     - ✅ Manage pages
     - ✅ Publish posts
     - ✅ Read insights
     - ✅ Manage comments (optional)

4. **Select Pages/Accounts**
   - For Facebook/LinkedIn: Choose which pages to connect
   - For Twitter/Instagram: Confirm account selection

5. **Verify Connection**
   - You should see a green checkmark next to the connected account
   - Account name and avatar will display

### Troubleshooting Connections

**Issue**: "Unable to connect account"
- **Solution**: Ensure you're an admin of the page/account
- **Solution**: Check that pop-ups are enabled in your browser
- **Solution**: Try clearing browser cache and reconnecting

**Issue**: "Permissions denied"
- **Solution**: Review and grant all required permissions during authorization
- **Solution**: Re-authenticate by clicking **Reconnect** on the account card

## ✍️ Creating Your First Post

### Step-by-Step Post Creation

#### 1. Navigate to Create Post Tab
- Click **Create Post** in the top navigation

#### 2. Select Target Accounts
- Check the boxes next to accounts where you want to publish
- You can select multiple accounts for cross-posting
- **Tip**: Different platforms have different character limits—adjust content accordingly

#### 3. Compose Your Content

**Text Content:**
- Enter your post text in the main editor
- Character counts display for each selected platform:
  - Facebook: 63,206 characters
  - Twitter: 280 characters
  - LinkedIn: 3,000 characters
  - Instagram: 2,200 characters
- **Tip**: Write for the shortest limit (Twitter) if cross-posting

**Add Media:**
- Click **Upload Media** or drag files into the dropzone
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, MOV
- Max file size: 25MB per file
- You can upload multiple images/videos
- **Reorder media**: Drag thumbnails to rearrange

**Add Hashtags:**
- Type `#` to trigger hashtag suggestions
- Click **AI Suggestions** for context-aware hashtags
- **Best Practice**: Use 3-5 relevant hashtags per post

#### 4. Preview Your Post
- Toggle **Preview Mode** to see how your post will appear on each platform
- Check formatting, media display, and character counts
- Make adjustments as needed

#### 5. Choose Publishing Option

**Publish Now:**
- Click **Publish Now** to post immediately
- Confirm the accounts in the dialog
- Click **Confirm** to publish

**Schedule for Later:**
- Click **Schedule**
- Select date and time using the date/time picker
- Set timezone (defaults to your account timezone)
- Click **Schedule Post**

**Save as Draft:**
- Click **Save Draft**
- Draft auto-saves every 30 seconds
- Access drafts from the **Posts** tab

**Submit for Approval:**
- Click **Submit for Approval**
- Select approvers from the dropdown
- Add optional notes for reviewers
- Click **Submit**

## 📅 Scheduling & Calendar Management

### Calendar View

**Navigating the Calendar:**
- Click **Calendar** tab to view monthly schedule
- Use arrow buttons to navigate months
- Hover over posts to see preview
- Click a post to edit or view details

### Drag-to-Reschedule

**Rescheduling Posts:**
1. Click and hold a scheduled post in the calendar
2. Drag to the new date
3. Release to drop
4. Select new time in the time picker dialog
5. Click **Confirm**

**Bulk Rescheduling:**
1. Enable multi-select mode (checkbox in top-right)
2. Check boxes next to posts you want to reschedule
3. Click **Bulk Schedule**
4. Choose new date/time
5. Click **Apply to Selected**

### Recurring Posts

**Setting Up Recurring Posts:**
1. Create your post as usual
2. Click **Schedule**
3. Toggle **Recurring Post**
4. Select frequency:
   - Daily
   - Weekly (select days)
   - Monthly (select dates)
   - Custom interval
5. Set start and end dates (or ongoing)
6. Click **Schedule Recurring**

**Managing Recurring Posts:**
- Edit one instance: Changes apply to that instance only
- Edit all instances: Click **Edit Series** and select **All Future**

## ✅ Approval Workflows

### Understanding Roles

**Creator:**
- Creates posts and submits for approval
- Can save drafts and view submitted posts
- Cannot publish without approval

**Reviewer:**
- Reviews submitted posts
- Can approve or request changes
- Cannot publish (requires Approver)

**Approver:**
- Final approval authority
- Can approve posts for publishing
- Can override previous rejections

**Admin:**
- Full access to all features
- Can bypass approval workflow
- Manages user roles and permissions

### Submitting for Approval

1. **Create your post** (see [Creating Your First Post](#creating-your-first-post))
2. Click **Submit for Approval**
3. Select reviewers/approvers from dropdown
4. Add notes (optional): "Please review by EOD Friday"
5. Click **Submit**
6. **Email notification** sent to reviewers

### Reviewing Posts

**As a Reviewer:**
1. Check your email for approval notification
2. Click link to view post in platform
3. Review content, media, and targeting
4. Choose action:
   - **Approve**: Move to next approval level
   - **Request Changes**: Add comments and send back to creator
   - **Reject**: Cancel post with reason

**Adding Review Comments:**
- Click **Add Comment** in the review panel
- Type your feedback
- Click **Submit Comment**
- Creator receives email notification with comments

### Approval History

- View approval history in the **Posts** tab
- Filter by approval status:
  - ⏳ Pending Approval
  - ✅ Approved
  - ❌ Rejected
  - 🔄 Changes Requested
- Click any post to see full approval timeline

## 📈 Analytics & Performance Tracking

### Analytics Dashboard

**Key Metrics:**
- **Total Reach**: Number of unique users who saw your content
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares per post
- **Impressions**: Total number of times content was displayed
- **Click-Through Rate**: Percentage of users who clicked links

**Filters:**
- Date range: Last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, custom range
- Account filter: View metrics for specific accounts
- Post type: Organic vs. scheduled posts

### Hashtag Analytics

**Tracking Hashtag Performance:**
1. Navigate to **Analytics** tab
2. Scroll to **Hashtag Performance** section
3. View metrics per hashtag:
   - **Reach**: Users who saw posts with this hashtag
   - **Engagement**: Interactions on posts with this hashtag
   - **Posts**: Number of posts using this hashtag
4. Sort by:
   - Top performers (highest engagement)
   - Trending (fastest growth)
   - Recent (newest hashtags)

**Trend Charts:**
- **Performance Over Time**: Line chart showing hashtag engagement trends
- **Comparison View**: Compare up to 5 hashtags side-by-side
- **Seasonal Trends**: Identify peak performance periods

### Exporting Analytics

1. Click **Export** button in Analytics tab
2. Select export format:
   - CSV (spreadsheet)
   - PDF (report)
   - JSON (raw data)
3. Choose data to include:
   - All metrics
   - Custom selection
4. Click **Download**

## 🤖 AI-Powered Features

### Performance Prediction

**Before You Publish:**
- AI predicts engagement score (0-100)
- Prediction based on:
  - Time of day
  - Day of week
  - Hashtag quality
  - Content length
  - Historical performance
- **Green score (80+)**: Excellent timing and content
- **Yellow score (50-79)**: Good, minor optimizations suggested
- **Red score (<50)**: Consider revising or rescheduling

**Using Predictions:**
1. Create your post
2. Click **Analyze** to run prediction
3. Review score and suggestions
4. Apply AI recommendations:
   - Suggested hashtags
   - Optimal posting time
   - Content improvements
5. Re-analyze after changes

### AI Hashtag Suggestions

**Getting Suggestions:**
1. In post composer, type your content
2. Click **AI Suggestions** under hashtag field
3. AI analyzes your content and suggests:
   - Trending hashtags in your niche
   - High-performing hashtags from your history
   - Related hashtags based on content
4. Click hashtags to add them to your post
5. **Limit**: Max 30 hashtags (Instagram), 5-10 recommended

**Customizing Suggestions:**
- **Industry**: Set your industry in Brand Voice settings
- **Tone**: Formal, casual, playful, professional
- **Target Audience**: Demographics and interests

### Brand Voice AI

**Setting Up Brand Voice:**
1. Navigate to **Brand Voice** tab
2. Click **Create New Voice Profile**
3. Define your brand:
   - **Company Name**: Your business name
   - **Industry**: E.g., "Real Estate," "Tech Startup"
   - **Tone**: Select 2-3 descriptors (friendly, professional, humorous)
   - **Key Messages**: Core values and themes
   - **Sample Posts**: Upload 3-5 example posts
4. Click **Train AI Model** (takes 2-3 minutes)
5. **Activate** profile for post generation

**Using Brand Voice:**
1. In post composer, click **Generate with AI**
2. Enter a topic or brief: "New product launch announcement"
3. Select your Brand Voice profile
4. Click **Generate**
5. AI creates 3 post variations matching your brand
6. Select one or edit as needed

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Content Strategy

**Posting Frequency:**
- **Facebook**: 1-2 posts per day
- **Twitter**: 3-5 tweets per day
- **LinkedIn**: 1 post per weekday
- **Instagram**: 1-2 posts per day

**Optimal Posting Times:**
- **B2B Audiences**: Weekdays 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM (EST)
- **B2C Audiences**: Evenings 6-9 PM, weekends 9 AM-12 PM
- **Tip**: Use AI Performance Prediction to find your best times

**Content Mix (60-30-10 Rule):**
- **60%** Educational/Valuable content
- **30%** Engaging/Entertaining content
- **10%** Promotional content

### Media Best Practices

**Image Specifications:**
- **Facebook**: 1200x630px (landscape), 1080x1080px (square)
- **Twitter**: 1200x675px (landscape)
- **LinkedIn**: 1200x627px (landscape)
- **Instagram**: 1080x1080px (square), 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG, <5MB for best performance

**Video Specifications:**
- **Length**: 30-60 seconds (optimal engagement)
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Captions**: Always include (80% watch without sound)

### Hashtag Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- **Quantity**: 3-5 hashtags per post (quality over quantity)
- **Mix**: Combine popular (#marketing) with niche (#B2BContentStrategy)
- **Branded**: Create and use a branded hashtag (#YourCompanyName)
- **Avoid**: Overused generic hashtags (#love, #instagood)
- **Research**: Use Hashtag Analytics to identify top performers

### Approval Workflow Best Practices

**For Creators:**
- Submit posts 24-48 hours before desired publish time
- Include context in submission notes
- Address reviewer comments promptly
- Keep drafts organized by campaign or theme

**For Reviewers:**
- Review submissions within 24 hours
- Provide constructive, specific feedback
- Use approval templates for common feedback
- Communicate directly for urgent posts

**For Approvers:**
- Set clear approval criteria and communicate to team
- Delegate review tasks when possible
- Use bulk approval for similar posts
- Monitor approval bottlenecks and adjust workflow

### Crisis Management

**Handling Negative Comments:**
1. **Respond within 1 hour** during business hours
2. **Acknowledge the issue**: "Thank you for bringing this to our attention."
3. **Move to private**: "Please DM us so we can resolve this."
4. **Follow up publicly**: "We've resolved this issue with [user]."

**Post Mistakes:**
1. **Delete immediately** if factual error or offensive
2. **Post correction**: Acknowledge error and provide correct info
3. **Learn**: Document in team notes to prevent recurrence

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Post failed to publish"
**Possible Causes:**
- Account token expired
- Platform API rate limit reached
- Media file too large or unsupported format

**Solutions:**
1. Reconnect the account in **Accounts** tab
2. Wait 15 minutes and retry (rate limit reset)
3. Reduce media file size or convert format

#### "Calendar not loading posts"
**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (Cmd/Ctrl + R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check date range filter (expand to wider range)

#### "AI suggestions not working"
**Solutions:**
1. Ensure post content is >20 characters
2. Check internet connection
3. Retry in a few minutes (API timeout)

#### "Unable to drag-drop media"
**Solutions:**
1. Use Chrome or Firefox (Safari has limited support)
2. Click **Upload** button as alternative
3. Check file permissions (ensure files aren't locked)

### Browser Compatibility

**Recommended Browsers:**
- ✅ **Chrome** 90+ (best performance)
- ✅ **Firefox** 88+
- ✅ **Safari** 14+ (limited drag-drop)
- ❌ Internet Explorer (not supported)

**Enable JavaScript and Cookies:**
- Platform requires JavaScript enabled
- Cookies must be allowed for authentication
